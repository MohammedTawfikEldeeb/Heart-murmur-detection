Heart Murmur Detection

Welcome to the Heart Murmur Detection tool

About Heart Murmurs:

Heart murmurs are unusual sounds heard during a heartbeat. They’re often harmless but can sometimes indicate a serious heart condition. Early detection is crucial for effective treatment.

About the Project:

This project aims to use machine learning to detect heart murmurs from audio data. We’ve trained models that can identify the presence of a murmur with high accuracy.

My Methodology:

I’ve used several machine learning models, including Support Vector Machines (SVM), AdaBoost, and a Deep Neural Network. These models were trained and tested on a dataset of heart sounds.

Results:

Our models have achieved promising results, with accuracy rates above 98%. We’re continuously working to improve these results and make the models even better.

How to Record

When recording heart sounds using a smartphone, it’s important to follow these precautions to ensure the best quality recording:

Quiet Environment

Choose a quiet environment to minimize background noise.

Turn off any music or television, close the windows to block out street noise, and ask others to be quiet if necessary.

Proper Placement

Place the microphone of the phone on the chest, slightly to the left of the sternum (breastbone), like in the picture.

Steady Hold

Hold the phone steady during the recording to avoid any additional noise from movement. It might be helpful to rest your hand or arm on a table to keep it steady.

Avoid Touching the Microphone

Be careful not to touch or rub the microphone while recording as this can create additional noise.

How to Use

Our heart murmur detection tool is designed to be user-friendly, Here’s how you can use it:

Step 1:

Record your heart sound using the instructions provided above.

Step 2:

Save the recording in a .wav format or any other format compatible with our tool. We’re using the librosa library which supports a variety of audio formats including .wav, .mp3, .flac, and others.

Step 3:

Upload the audio file using the ‘Upload’ button on our website.

Step 4:

Click on ‘Analyze’ to run the heart murmur detection algorithm.

Step 5:

The results will be displayed on the screen. If a heart murmur is detected, please consult with a healthcare provider for further evaluation.

Remember, this tool is intended for research purposes and is not a substitute for professional medical advice.
